Philadelphia Farmers Markets

Philadelphia Farmers Markets are a staple to good health and community well-being. These markets are located all over the city and offer opportunities for locals and travelers to unite on the love of food. Often farmers markets are a hub for art and music. It's common to see artists show off their hard work and splash a color of culture to the already vibrant city of Philadelphia. Enjoy this interactive map and visit a farmers market near you!


Which Farmers Markets are Open on Saturday?

Farmers market are open on different days of the week, this makes it easy for everyone to find time to enjoy fresh produce, culture and meet new friends. Make sure to bring your umbrella though! Farmers markets are mostly outside and a sudden rain event can really dampen your mood. Pun intended.
